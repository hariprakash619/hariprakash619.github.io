About

Greetings, I am Hari Prakash Vel Murugan. My journey in the world of technology has been marked by a relentless pursuit of knowledge and a commitment to excellence.

My Background: I completed my B.Tech. in Computer Science at Vellore Institute of Technology in India in May 2018. I am currently pursuing my M.S. in Computer Science at Arizona State University, with an expected graduation date of December 2024.

Technical Proficiency: My toolkit includes a diverse range of programming languages such as Python, JavaScript, Java, SAP ABAP, and C/C++. During my time there, I delivered an Android app for the Mitra robot, implemented machine learning tools, and contributed to improving usability and functionality.
